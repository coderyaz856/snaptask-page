SnapTask — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026 · Last reviewed: May 3, 2026

SnapTask is an Android application developed by Quordesk Labs (Morocco). Website: snaptask.org. This policy explains what data the app collects, why it is collected, and how it is handled. We are the data controller for any personal data processed in connection with the app.

1. Data Stored Locally

All tasks, notes, tags, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device in a local database. This data is never uploaded to any server. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted unless you have created a backup.

2. Data Sent to Third Parties

For details on how these third parties handle data, see: Firebase Privacy, Gumroad Privacy, Ahrefs Privacy.

2.1 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) — crash reporting, anonymous app analytics, and website analytics, to maintain and improve app/site quality.
  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) — license key verification, to provide the Pro features you purchased.

2.2 International Data Transfers

Crash reports and analytics data may be transferred to and processed in the United States by Google LLC. Google participates in the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework. For details, see Firebase Privacy.

2.3 Opting Out of Analytics

You can limit analytics data collection by revoking internet permission for SnapTask in your device settings (Settings → Apps → SnapTask → Permissions → disable network access). This will also disable crash reporting and license verification. SnapTask does not currently offer an in-app analytics toggle; if this changes, this policy will be updated.

2.4 Advertising ID

SnapTask does not use the Android Advertising ID for ad targeting. Firebase Analytics may collect a device identifier for aggregated analytics purposes only. No advertising profiles are created.

4. Backups

When you create a backup, a copy of your local database is saved to a location you choose via the Android share sheet. Backups are never uploaded automatically. You control where your data goes.

5. Data Retention

Your tasks and settings remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Crash reports and anonymous analytics are retained by Google per their standard retention policies (typically 90 days for crash data, 14 months for analytics).

6. Children's Privacy

SnapTask is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data through the app, please contact us at ftgygh0@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such data.

7. Your Rights

Since your tasks and settings are stored locally on your device, you have full control over them at all times. You can delete tasks, export your data, or uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data. No account creation is required to use SnapTask.

7.1 Rights Under GDPR / UK GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights regarding the limited data processed by Firebase and Gumroad on our behalf:

  • Right of access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email ftgygh0@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Because SnapTask requires no account, we may need to ask for information (such as your Gumroad order ID or device crash identifier) to locate any data associated with you.

7.2 Rights Under CCPA / CPRA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected about you, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. SnapTask does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact ftgygh0@gmail.com. We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the limited data SnapTask processes:

  • All network traffic uses HTTPS / TLS — cleartext network traffic is blocked at the OS level via network_security_config.
  • Sensitive on-device values (such as your license key) are stored using the Android Keystore.
  • Release builds are obfuscated and shrunk with R8/ProGuard, and debug logging is stripped.
  • The app does not embed any web tracking SDKs, advertising SDKs, or social-media SDKs beyond Firebase.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to applying current Android best practices.

9. Data Breach Notification

If we become aware of a security incident affecting personal data processed in connection with SnapTask, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours where required by law (GDPR Article 33), and we will notify affected users without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new effective date. Material changes will be highlighted at the top of the document for at least 30 days.
